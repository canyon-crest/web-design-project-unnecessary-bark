Drums:

I first started playing the drums because one of my mom’s close friends invited me and mom over to dinner with her and her family. One of her son’s, Anthony, was about 6 years older than me and after we ate, he showed me around his electronic drum set and showed me how fast he could use the double kick that he had on his drum set. A double pedal is a second pedal right next to the pedal that closes the hi-hat that connects to an axle that connects to a batter in front of the kick pedal so along with the other standard pedal for the kick drum, you could play very fast. It’s typically just for heavy metal but he had one just for fun. I found it very interesting and fascinating but I didn’t really think about it again. A few weeks later, my mom asked me about what instrument I wanted to play and I said the drums and she got me into lessons. Not immediately though, we both forgot about it for a year or two. But once I got into it, it was a ton of fun. My favorite music artist is Tame Impala so I learned how to play two of his songs on the drums, Reality in Motion, and Music To Walk Home By. Next semester I’m taking rock band so I can play the drums for school!
